THE GENTLE ART

Jiu Jitsu, a.k.a the Gentle Art, is a martial art that is as elegant as it is effective. It's a form of self-defense that emphasizes leverage, technique, and fluidity of movement over brute strength. In fact, the beauty of Jiu Jitsu lies in its ability to empower individuals of all sizes and genders to defend themselves against larger and stronger opponents.





HISTORY

Jiu Jitsu originated in Japan as a practical combat system for samurai warriors, it was brought to Brazil and developed into the practice we know of today.





TECHNICAL

Technique is one of the fundamental pillars of Jiu Jitsu. By mastering the techniques of the gentle art, practitioners can learn to defend themselves effectively against larger, stronger opponents, using their opponent's own weight and momentum against them.





discipline

Jiu Jitsu teaches children and adults discipline from the moment they step on the mats. A form of helping students become better pupils and people, breeding resilience and respect.
